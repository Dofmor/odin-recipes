Tacos

Description

This is a recipe for homemade carne asada tacos

Ingrediants:

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Beef
    • Desired Spices
  • Salt
  • Cilantro
  • 1 Onion
  • Salsa

Steps:

  1. Dice onion and cilantro
  2. Add half the onion and the whole cilantro together in a small bowl. This will be your topping
  3. Rub salt and spices onto meat
  4. Cook beef on stove with left over diced onion
  5. Heat tortillas until their soft enough to not crumble when bent
  6. Add cooked meat and onion together onto the tortilla
  7. Add cilantro topping
  8. Add salsa
