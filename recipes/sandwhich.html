Sandwhich

Description:

A recipe on a basic ham sandwhich

Ingrediants:

  • 2 Slices of Wheat Bread
  • Ham
  • 1 Slice of Swiss Cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Lettuce
  • 1 Slice of Tomatoe

Steps:

  1. Toast the whole wheat bread
  2. Spread mayonaise on both slices of bread
  3. Add the ham, swiss cheese, lettuce, tomatoe in that order onto one of the slices of bread
  4. Finish it off by adding the last slice of whole wheat bread onto the tomatoe on top
